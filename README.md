## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I hold an MSc in Applied Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics from the **University of Bradford, UK**, and I work at the intersection of machine learning, advanced analytics, and real-world problem solving.

My focus is on building intelligent, end-to-end solutions — from exploratory data analysis and feature engineering through to model development and business reporting. I've built predictive models achieving **R² = 0.94**, analysed over **1.8M records** to uncover patterns that inform policy, and designed full-stack data applications from the ground up.

I'm drawn to problems where data can change how decisions are made — and I bring a rigorous analytical mindset, clean engineering practices, and genuine curiosity to every challenge.
